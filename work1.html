PAN

Personal Area Network

Typically within 10 meters

eg. Bluetooth, USB, Infrared

LAN

Local Area Network

Usually within 100 meters

Eg. Home network, Office network

WAN

Wide Area Network

Typically cover Multiple cities or large geograpical area

eg. Ethernet

MAN

Metropolitan Area Network

Typically cover large campus or area

Eg. city wide WI-FI
